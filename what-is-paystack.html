What is Paystack?

I've never heard of Paystack before, what is it?

Paystack is a payments platform that makes the online payments process seamless for both the consumers and the businesses they are trying to pay. We make easy for merchants to accept credit and debit card payments online from customers. Paystack launched in beta in January 2016.

Which companies are using Paystack?

Hotels.ng, Payporte, IrokoTV, Jobberman, Printivo, Gigalayer, amongst hundreds of other businesses selling to their customers online.

Is Paystack secure?

Yes. We're PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) certified. Our systems are audited by a Qualified Security Accessor and security extremely important to us. All communication is conducted via HTTPS using TLS 1.2 (Transport Layer Security).

I'm about to subscribe for recurring billing, how are my card details saved?

Your card details are securely tokenized and the tokens are used to charge you subsequently. In any case, your CVVs and PINs are never stored.
